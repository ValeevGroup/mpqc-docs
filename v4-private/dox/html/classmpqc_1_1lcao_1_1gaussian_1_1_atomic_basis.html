Detailed Description

AtomicBasis is a Basis constructed from a collection of atoms, i.e. a Molecule (or a UnitCell). Updating the Molecule object updates this basis as well. Clustering of AtomicBasis does not have to correspond to the cluster structure of Molecule (e.g. as a result of retiling; furthermore, Molecule is clustered recursively, whereas Basis is nonrecursive).

Warning
Shells currently appear in the order of increasing atom index (see Molecule::atoms() for the definition of atom index). However, this may change in the future and should not be assumed in new code.
