Accessor for occupation numbers of orbitals.

Parameters
saccess occupancies of the orbitals of this Spin case
Returns
the occpancies of the orbitals of spin case s ;
Note
corner case of spin-free/soin-restricted orbitals (i.e., nspincases() == 1):
  • if constructed with 2 spin-specific orbital attributes (e.g., for the spin-restricted case where orbitals are spin-independent but the occupation numbers are) requesting spin-free occupancies (s==Spin::None) returns the total occupancies.
  • if constructed with spin-free attributes but asked for spin-dependent occupancies (s!=Spin::None) assert that this is a closed-shell case, i.e. occupancies are 2 or 0, and convert to spin-specific occupancies (1 or 0).
