Detailed Description

template<typename Tile>
struct mpqc::lcao::detail::Mp2Energy< Tile >

Mp2Energy is a reduced for 2-particle perturbation energies that have form of \( \sum_{ijab} \frac{N^{ab}_{ij}}{D^{ab}_{ij}} \)
