Detailed Description

template<typename Basis>
class mpqc::SPSpace< Basis >

SPSpace represents a single-particle space supported by a basis.

This represents either a spin-free/spin-indeterminate or spin-specific basis (specified by a single set of functions or expansion coefficient matrices) or spin-aggregate LCAOs (specified by two sets of functions or expansion coefficient matrices).
