template<typename TileZ , typename Tile , typename Policy >
TA::DistArray< TileZ, Policy > mpqc::lcao::pbc::transform_real2recip ( const TA::DistArray< Tile, Policy > &  matrix,
Vector3d  vec_k,
const UnitCell &  unitcell,
const Supercell &  supercell 
)

Transform operator matrix from AO basis to Bloch-AO basis.

Can be viewed in one of two ways:

  • ket dimension is transformed to Bloch-AOs defined by \( \sum_\mathbf{R} \exp(\mathrm{i} \mathbf{k} \cdot \mathbf{R}) \phi_{mu \mathbf{R}} \) ; this is how the transform is implemented.
  • bra and ket dimensions are transformed to Bloch-AOs defined by \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{N}} \sum_\mathbf{R} \exp(\mathrm{i} \mathbf{k} \cdot \mathbf{R}) \phi_{mu \mathbf{R}} \), where \( N \) is the size of the supercell.
    Template Parameters
    TileZthe complex-valued TA tile type
    Tilethe real-valued TA tile type
    Policythe TA policy type
    Parameters
    [in]matrixan operator matrix expressed in terms of AOs in a supercell
    [in]vec_kthe target k vector for the Bloch AOs
    [in]unitcellthe (direct) unit cell
    [in]supercellthe supercell used to express Bloch AOs
    Returns
    matrix in the Bloch-AO basis
